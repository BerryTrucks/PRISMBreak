The PRISM Break Secure Email application does not transmit any personal data via Internet or through any other means of electronic communication. It only uses e-mail accounts for the purpose of sending and receiving secure and password protected messages.

Required permissions:

  • The Device Identifying Information permission is required for the application to surpass the limitations of the pseudo-random generator by applying additional safeguards to increase the security of your private and public keys.
  • Access to Email and PIN Messages, Contacts and My Contact Info is required to enable the application to send, receive and read email messages.
  • The Shared Files permission is required for the successful importing and exporting of secure files.

If you have any concerns regarding privacy and security, please don't hesitate to contact us at prismbreak@pronic.si.
